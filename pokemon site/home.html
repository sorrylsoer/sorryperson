ABOUT THE GAME ITSELF!

League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Riot Games.

Gameplay

League of Legends features strategic and fast-paced gameplay where two teams, each consisting of five powerful champions, compete to destroy the opposing team's Nexus.

Champions

Explore a vast roster of over 150 unique champions, each with their own abilities and playstyles. From fierce warriors to powerful mages and cunning assassins, League of Legends offers a diverse selection of champions to suit different playstyles and preferences.
